About DFC Self Photo Studio

Where everyday moments become frame-worthy portraits.

Our Story

DFC Self-Photo Studio is a self-shoot studio based in Poblacion, San Rafael, Bulacan. We’re all about giving you a fun, relaxed space where you can pose, laugh, and be yourself— whether you’re solo, with your partner, your barkada, or your whole family.

With studio lighting, colorful backdrops, and an easy self-photo setup, you stay in control while still getting photos you’ll be proud to share and keep.

What We Believe

  • Every milestone deserves to be celebrated and remembered.
  • Photoshoots should feel fun, not intimidating.
  • Great lighting + a comfortable space = confident photos.
