ABOUT DFC SELF‑PHOTO STUDIO

Fun. Creative. 100% You.

😄 Who We Are

DFC Self‑Photo Studio is a cozy, creative space where you can laugh, pose, experiment, and capture your best moments — your way.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, bonding with friends, or just feeling cute today, our studio gives you the freedom to express yourself without pressure or awkwardness.

We’re proudly located in Poblacion, San Rafael, Bulacan, serving the community with a studio that’s bright, comfortable, and full of good vibes.

📸 What Makes Us Fun

✨ You’re the photographer — Use the remote shutter and take photos at your own pace.

🎨 Choose your mood — Cute, dramatic, fierce, wholesome — whatever your vibe is today.

💡 Studio‑grade lighting — Clean, crisp, flattering shots every time.

👯 Perfect for everyone — Solo shoots, couples, besties, families, barkada — we love it all.

🎬 Your Studio Experience

1. Pick your backdrop — Choose the color or theme that matches your mood.

2. Get comfy — We’ll help you settle in and guide you through the setup.

3. Shoot your way — Use the remote shutter and have fun — pose, laugh, experiment.

4. Review your shots — Choose your favorites right after the session.

💛 Our Promise

We’re here to make sure you walk out with photos that feel like you — fun, expressive, and full of life.

Whether it’s a milestone or a random Tuesday, your moments deserve to be captured beautifully.
